Church Website

Description

Status: Finished


In July, a church committee meeting was held where they were discussing on how they needed a church website and need it quickly so they can inugurate it by the head bishop that would be coming in a few weeks. However, they didn't have the funds to hire a web developer. I overheard this and volunteered to create them a website for free. In the first week I made a skeleton of the website and showed it to the committee members. After the first week I adjusted the website so that it fit the needs of the church members by adding a subscription box a timeline and a events page that had a countdown. This were some of the modifications I made at the request of the church members. When I finished I gave a quick talk on what the website was about to the whole church.
